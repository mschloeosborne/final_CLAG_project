ABOUT US.

Hello, we are going solo. A online meeting place for female, non-gender conforming and non-binary individuals who are passionate about travelling on their own terms. We are passionate about enabling and empowering solo for individuals across the world. Join us for tips, photos and blogs to help faciliate your trip today. Wether you would like to travel to urban centres or off the beaten path, we have tips and support for you! Contact us today for specialised female advice and assistance with flights, visas, tips and more.

OUR MISSION & GOALS.

Did you know that single women are the largest growing travelling demographics? Our mission is to empower 1000 female solos adventures. By closing the travel gap, we hope to inspire women to travel the world, while using essential safety tips. Our team is passionate about securing the future of female travel, and hope to help women across the globe find themselves though travel.

HOW WE ACHIEVE THIS.

To achieve our mission, we offer women services which can facilitate their solo adventures. We are not your mothers travel agency! With a focus on local knowledge and a team who have seen it all, we can help get you there sooner! We offer visa consultations, flight bookings, luggage tips, safety information and more to ensure your trip is memorable for the right reasons!
